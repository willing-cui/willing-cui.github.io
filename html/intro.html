Intro

My academic journey has been shaped by a deep curiosity and a desire to contribute meaningfully to the fields of information engineering and emerging technologies. I am currently pursuing a Ph.D. in Information Engineering at The Chinese University of Hong Kong (36th in QS), under the supervision of Professor Soung Chang LIEW. My research explores areas such as optical wireless communication, routing algorithms, LLM-based AI agents, and AI-powered wireless communication and IoT systems — topics I find both challenging and inspiring, and to which I aim to contribute through my work.

Before embarking on my Ph.D., I earned my Bachelor’s degree in Electronic Information Engineering at Shandong University, one of China’s top universities. Through a competitive selection process, I was admitted to Chongxin College, an advanced program within the department that offered a broader curriculum and rigorous experimental requirements. There, I worked diligently to achieve a GPA of 4.625/5.0, ranking 1st in my class. This experience not only provided me with a solid foundation in engineering principles but also taught me the value of perseverance and continuous learning.
