Intro

My academic journey has been shaped by a deep curiosity and a desire to contribute meaningfully to the fields of information engineering and emerging technologies I am currently pursuing a Ph.D. in Information Engineering at The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), a globally recognized institution ranked within the top 50 (#36) in the QS World University Rankings, under the supervision of Professor Soung Chang LIEW. My research explores areas such as optical wireless communication, routing algorithms, LLM-based AI agents, and AI-powered wireless communication and IoT systems — topics I find both challenging and inspiring, and to which I aim to contribute through my work.

Before embarking on my Ph.D., I earned my Bachelor’s degree in Electronic Information Engineering at Shandong University, one of China’s top universities. Through a competitive selection process, I was admitted to Chongxin College, an advanced program within the department that offered a broader curriculum and rigorous experimental requirements. There, I worked diligently to achieve a GPA of 4.625/5.0, ranking first in my class. This experience not only provided me with a solid foundation in engineering principles but also taught me the value of perseverance and continuous learning.

I feel fortunate to have had the opportunity to study at such esteemed institutions and to learn from inspiring mentors and peers along the way. My education has instilled in me a sense of responsibility to use my skills and knowledge to address real-world challenges, and I remain committed to approaching my work with humility and a willingness to grow.
